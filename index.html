Urban spaces aren't just leftover land.
They're opportunities waiting for the right idea.

EventSeek shows that even in a highly planned city like Singapore, small, irregular leftover spaces persist — and each one has potential for the right kind of activation. EventSeek's search engine approach means every match is data-driven — not hand-picked but algorithmically discovered. Based on the analysis of 1,197 Street View panoramas across Jalan Besar, clustered into 5 spatial typologies via Self-Organizing Map.
